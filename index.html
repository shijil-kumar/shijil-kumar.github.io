Selected case studies

Kiora AmorezJewellery
01 — Kiora Amorez

Fine jewellery that sells on desire

Jewellery · Calicut
Problem

Fine jewellery converts on desire and trust. A generic catalogue template flattens the pieces and loses buyers before they ever reach an enquiry.

Approach

An editorial, gallery-first layout with generous negative space, slow scroll reveals, and one unmistakable enquiry path — so every gemstone stays the hero.

Result

A boutique that feels as considered as the pieces it sells: mobile-perfect, fast, and built to turn quiet browsing into real enquiries.

Stack: Shopify/WooCommerce · custom theme · CRO · GSAP · mobile-first

02 — Keemz Medical

Credibility in seconds for B2B buyers

B2B · Medical · Dubai
Problem

Medical procurement teams need to trust a supplier fast and find the right equipment without friction — hesitation costs the enquiry.

Approach

A clean, credibility-first structure: clear category paths, spec-forward product blocks, and a prominent quote / contact CTA on every screen.

Result

A supplier presence that reads established and organised, guiding buyers straight from browsing to a quote request.

Stack: Shopify/WooCommerce · custom theme · CRO · GSAP · mobile-first
